Grilled Fish Steaks

Description

Beautifully simple grilled fish recipe perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) fillets halibut

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper together in a stainless steel or glass bowl.
  3. Place the halibut fillets in a shallow glass dish or a resealable plastic bag; pour the marinade over the fish. Cover or seal and place in the refrigerator for 1 hour, turning occasionally.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate. Set grate 4 inches from the heat.
  5. Remove halibut fillets from marinade and drain off any excess. Cook on the preheated grill until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes per side.
